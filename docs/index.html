Actual Gym

A modern platform for gym management.

The Problem

Gym management shouldn't be a workout in itself

For Gym Managers

  • Juggling spreadsheets for member tracking and scheduling
  • Manual payment tracking leading to errors and delays
  • Difficulty managing multiple coaches and their schedules
  • Lack of real-time insights into gym operations
  • Time-consuming administrative tasks reducing focus on members

For Gym Members

  • Complicated registration and onboarding processes
  • Unclear session availability and booking procedures
  • Payment confusion and lack of transparency
  • Limited visibility into their membership status
  • Poor communication with gym staff and coaches

Our Solution

Streamlined gym management, empowered members

Actual Gym is a comprehensive platform that bridges the gap between gym operations and member experience. We provide an intuitive, all-in-one solution that automates administrative tasks, simplifies payment processing, and creates a seamless experience for both managers and members.

Real-time operations management
Automated payment processing with Stripe
Easy member and coach management
Intuitive booking and scheduling system
